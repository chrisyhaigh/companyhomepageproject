About Us

Since starting in 2016 we have hosted hundreds of customers on our world famous Taxi tours of our fine city Edinburgh . The tours are hosted by our lead guide Chrisy Haigh who has an extensive knowledge of the city and has vast experience of driving around the streets of Edinburgh and beyond. Whilst Edinburgh is our primary focus we also cover many other popular tourist destinations within Scotland itself such as Skye, St Andrews and much more.
